Lasagna for Garfield

Description

A lasagna is Garfield's favourite dish and is basically some meat, perhaps gabagool. Mostly used is beef mince and sometimes pork mince, my good friend todun also slow cooked a tomahawk. Then this mince is cooked with vegetables and herbs and spices.

Once this is cooked, you layer meat, pasta sheets, bechamel sauce, ad infinitum until you hit the top of your cooking dish.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Beef mince (500g)
  • Onion
  • Garlic cloves
  • Celery heads
  • Carrots
  • Tinned tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Ready-made bechamel sauce (family size

Steps

  1. Brown the mince with some oil
  2. Add chopped onions, celery, carrots
  3. Pour in 2 tins of tomatoes
  4. Bring to the boil and simmer until sauce looks rich
  5. Heat oven to 180C/Gas 4/Fan 160C
  6. Spoon some meat sauce in then layer pasta sheets
  7. Layer meat, pasta sheet, bechamel, repeat
  8. Oven cook for about 45mins
