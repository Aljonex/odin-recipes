Poverty stew

Description

This is an ingredient for poverty stew coined by Nicholas Dualibi-Wilson. On his stovetop whilst camping and is a delicious heart-warming one potter where many things can be added or taken away based on what you want. You can happily fluff and replace things to change it completely.

Ingredients

  • One onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 3 stalks of celery
  • Pack of sausages (herbier the better)
  • Tinned tomatoes
  • Tinned kidney beans
  • Tinned butter beans
  • Tinned cannelini beans
  • Quarter bottle of wine (double if ingredients double)

Steps

  1. Chop all veg
  2. Brown off onions, garlic, celery, carrots
  3. Add tin of tomatoes
  4. Add all tins of beans
  5. Stir
  6. Bring to the boil and let it simmer
  7. Add wine
  8. Cook some rice or pasta to go with it
